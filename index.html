Timeline 2008-2025

2008

Global Financial Crisis

One of the worst financial crises since the Great Depression began with the collapse of Lehman Brothers, leading to a global economic downturn.

2010

iPad Released

Apple released the first iPad, revolutionizing the tablet market and changing how people consume digital content.

2012

Higgs Boson Discovery

Scientists at CERN announced the discovery of the Higgs Boson particle, confirming a major piece of the Standard Model of physics.

2014

World Cup in Brazil

Brazil hosted the FIFA World Cup, which was won by Germany after they defeated Argentina in the final.

2016

Paris Climate Agreement

The landmark international accord was adopted to address climate change and its negative impacts, with nearly all countries signing on.

2018

First Photo of a Black Hole

The Event Horizon Telescope team captured the first-ever image of a black hole, located in the center of galaxy M87.

2020

COVID-19 Pandemic

The world faced an unprecedented global health crisis with the spread of the novel coronavirus, leading to lockdowns worldwide.

2022

James Webb Space Telescope

NASA's James Webb Space Telescope began operations, providing unprecedented views of distant galaxies and exoplanets.

2024

U.S. Presidential Election

Donald Trump elected as the 47th President of the United States, returning to office after a previous term.

2025

Present Day

The world continues to evolve with advances in AI, renewable energy, and space exploration shaping our future.
